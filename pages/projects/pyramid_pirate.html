  • Engine: Godot
  • Project Type: Game Jam

Pyramid Pirate is a game I made for the Spring 2024 UNCC Game Jam. The game won 1st in the "Overall" and "Innovation" categories.

Pyramid Pirate is a top-down roguelike with grid-based movement and turn-based combat. Players can avoid or attack enemies, collect gold, and find a key to the exit in each level. Each level is randomly generated using a walker algorithm, with items and enemies randomly placed onto available tiles.

Every turn, the player can move one grid space or use an attack. Enemies move once every third turn, and can attack the player when in an adjacent tile. To attack enemies, the player must collect a throwing knife from somewhere in the level. When used, the throwing knife moves one grid space per turn in the direction that the player last moved.
