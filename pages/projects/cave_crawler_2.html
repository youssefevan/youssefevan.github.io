// Cave Crawler 2 - Fall 2024

  • Role: Solo Developer
  • Engine: Godot
  • Project Type: Personal
  • Platform: Steam


Cave Crawler 2 is my second Steam release, which I published on October 9th, 2024. The game is a side-scrolling platformer with gameplay influence from 2D Mario and Mega Man games. In the game, you explore a series of caves and defeat 3 bosses to escape the underground.

Going into development, my goal was to expand the scope of the first game in the series and provide players with a level editor to create and share their own levels. Though the base mechanics are simple, each new level introduces a new enemy or game mechanic, so the gameplay stays interesting throughout. Each hazard and enemy behaves simply and predictably so the player can plan their immediate actions easily.

The depth of the game comes from combining these simple obstacles to create novel problems for the player to navigate. This design philosophy helped me through the level design process, which I struggled with during the development of the first Cave Crawler.
