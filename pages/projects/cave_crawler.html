• // Evan Youssef
// Cave Crawler - Spring 2022

  • Role: Solo Developer
  • Engine: Godot
  • Project Type: Graduation Project
  • Platform: Steam


Cave Crawler is my first Steam release and was created as a part of my high school graduation project. I released the game on January 23rd, 2023. Since launch, the game has earned 95% positive reviews and over 100,000 downloads.

Cave Crawler is the first game that I developed to completion, and the development process taught me a lot about project management. Before this point in my game development journey, I had spent many years making prototypes and half-finished projects but could never commit to developing an entire game. However, since my graduation project had a hard deadline, I finally gained the motivation to complete a game. Through completing Cave Crawler, I learned how to direct my motivation towards achieving a goal.

Although the game is small in scope, I am proud of how it turned out, and I'm grateful for its generous reception on Steam. Being my first finished game, the game has a lot of flaws, especially within the code. I tried my best to address the game's problem in Cave Crawler 2, which I released in October of 2024. I think the sequel illustrates how much I've grown as a developer in the past few years, and I'm excited to work on more long-term projects in the next few years.
