Cave Crawler

My first published video game was Cave Crawler, which was published on Steam in January of 2022. The game recieved over 100,000 downloads and earned 95% positive reviews. I created and published the game as a part of my high school graduation project.

Since then, I have competed in multiple Game Jam competitions, and have created several small projects that are all published on Itch.io.

Currently, I am working on a sequel to Cave Crawler that features a custom level editor along with new levels, enemies, and boss fights. You can view the source code on Github. I am aiming to publish Cave Crawler 2 on Steam in 2024.
